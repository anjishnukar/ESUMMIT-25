    How to treat your gear right?

    The football club's shirt is like their signature dish. Our upcycling method keeps it recognizable, respecting its sentimental value, while also caring for the environment by creating new from ‘old’.

    Up-grading

    re-cycling

    upcycling

    We’ll work with you to decide which parts of the original stock to keep. This way, each sample gives a great first look at the entire collection to be delivered.

    “Isn't the gear owned by our apparel partner?”

    *Foul whistle*

    Sports apparel, like football shirts, are allowed to get hand-cut, expertly stitched together, to create our exciting new upcycled products. As long as it’s explicit clear to recipients that new products are modified.

    Consistent and unique

    Products made from the same stock share a common base, ensuring consistency in elements like logo placement and design. However, because each item is handmade, every product remains unique and special.

    You get the best of both worlds!

    Uniformity in looks with a one-of-a-kind touch!

    Local craftsmanship based in...

    FC88's upcycled products are crafted locally in one of our seven studios across Europe, supporting regional craftsmanship.We strategically choose production locations based on their proximity our partners and available production capacity, ensuring sustainability and efficiency in every step.

    Tottenham Hotspur

    Earth Day 2023, FC88 created a bespoke THFC collection

    In collaboration with the club and Nike, FC88 also hosted an upcycle workshop for THFC fans. The bespoke THFC upcycled collection not only showed bags and hats, but also limited apparel items like button-down blouses. The involvement of players from the first mens and womens team, maximized the impact of the event and collection.

    UEFA

    UEFA and FC88 Partner to Upcycle
    Champions League Materials

    FC88 proudly partners with UEFA to upcycle event materials from the Champions League, Europa League, and Conference League. Banners, bibs, and other items will be transformed into high-quality products, reducing waste. All upcycling is done in Europe, supporting local craftsmanship and sustainability. FC88’s seven European studios ensure local production, minimizing transport emissions. The repurposed materials will become unique items, reflecting the spirit of football and UEFA's commitment to sustainability.
